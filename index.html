Types of Wallpaper

Wallpapers do not lose their popularity in the decoration of flats and houses. They are practical, not as expensive as some of the materials (plaster, wood), it's easy to glue, many kinds of wall-paper does not require pre-treatment of the substrate, the surface alignment. Correctly selected texture and the pattern of this coverage allows you to hide the shortcomings of surfaces of walls. There is a large selection of wall-paper, and textile Wallpaper on fabric basis, and non-woven (vinyl) with the content of polyvinylchloride weight. But the most common is the wall-paper, which may be as smooth or relief surface, single-or consist of two sheets, glued together.

The gluing of wall-paper

To qualitatively to paste wall-paper on a wall, you need to prepare the base, if the walls were previously covered by them, you need to remove the old cover. In cases when the wall surface for the first time pasted over such a floor, it must prepare and make it smooth. To Wallpaper kept stronger on a surface layer of ground, and on it is glued with a special paper. At a label of wall-paper on a wall it is necessary to consider features of each type of Wallpaper and have the appropriate skill, as well as wet Wallpaper become soft, lose their strength and may tear.
